About the School

Krim Belkacem is located in Tizi Ouzou, Draa Ben Khedda. It was established in 1988 and has a team of dedicated teachers and hardworking students.

Our Goals

  • Achieving the highest percentage of successful students.
  • Building a new generation based on education and responsibility.
  • Providing students with the appropriate amount of information on various topics.
  • Developing students’ awareness of exercising their duties and rights within the limits of their age and the characteristics of the stage they are going through.
  • Consolidating correct humanitarian principles in the hearts of students, and providing them with intellectual and physical education.
